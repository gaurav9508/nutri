HOW IT WORKS?

Our recommendation engine uses cutting-edge algorithms and agronomic principles to analyze your soil’s NPK levels and match them with the nutrient needs of your chosen crop. By entering just a few key details, you receive a personalized fertilizer plan designed to maximize yield and maintain soil fertility.

Example Scenario: For example, if your soil is low in nitrogen but high in phosphorus, our system will suggest a nitrogen-rich fertilizer while reducing or eliminating the need for additional phosphorus. This balanced approach not only enhances your crop’s growth but also conserves resources and minimizes environmental impact.
